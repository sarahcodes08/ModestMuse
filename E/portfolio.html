    Our Values

    Empower

    Our mission is to empower women to have choices to help build a life full of passion and purpose, whatever that may be. We are firm believers that employment is the key to financial independence, and financial independence provides freedom of choice.

    Community

    We are proud to have contributed financially towards their modular wardrobe initiative, with the target of 100 modular wardrobes to be built across women's and children's shelters across the country. Our DISSH team will be supporting Thread Together in building these wardrobes across Pakistan.

We take accountability for being part of an industry which has a huge impact on the environment and we are committed to major positive change.
